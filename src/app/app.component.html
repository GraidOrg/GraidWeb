The Problem: Professors Can't Personalize Learning

Professors struggle to personalize learning according to student needs because they don't know what everyone's individual needs are. We spoke with over 200 professors and they see AI's potential to personalize learning, but they can't influence how students use the AI.

Research shows that students who use AI effectively can get better test scores, but MIT research showed that students misusing AI leads to less brain activity.

The Solution: Graid
